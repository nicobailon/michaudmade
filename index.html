About

I have always loved to build and create with my own hands. With a background in woodworking and design, combined with a love for music, building musical instruments became a natural calling for me. Working solo as a Luthier out of my shop I craft guitars with a modern aesthetic while having a strong respect for tradition. Building guitars has become my way of life, and I live each day excited to create, learn and evolve. I hope to have the chance to build you your dream Michaud guitar.
